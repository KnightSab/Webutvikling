Tromsø, Paris of the north

Be it the stunning northern lights, the arctic wilderness, or scenic mountainscapes, a visit to Tromsø will be one to remember. Choose between two extremes, a never setting sun in summer, or no sun in winter. Everyone is welcome to visit, book a flight today!

Tromsø lies in Northern Norway, 69° north. With apopulation of 77,500, it's the 12th-most populous city in Norway. Here you can find city and nature activities, for example you can visit the worlds most northern distillery.

The city centre contains the highest number of old wooden houses in Northern Norway, the oldest dating from 1789. Tromsø is a cultural hub for the region, with several festivals taking place in the summer. Due to its location, many countries had consulates or missions in Tromsø in the 1990s.

Founded in 1836, the area has been inhabited since the end of the last ice age. Archeological excavations in Tønsvika, just outside the city limits, have turned up artifacts and remains of buildings estimated to be 9,000 to 10,000 years old.
